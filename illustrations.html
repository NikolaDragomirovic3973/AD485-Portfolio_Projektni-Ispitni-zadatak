ABOUT OUR ILLUSTRATIONS

"Our illustrations are always made by themselves, we are just a medium that is used to bring them to life". Funny but it's true. Our illustrations usually come from the depth of our hearts and they are always something new, refreshing, and creative. Characters are 100% original and they all have their own life behind their paintings. Every character has a life, friends, enemies, a home, and some cool abilities, or maybe they are just humans with very interesting advantages behind them.
